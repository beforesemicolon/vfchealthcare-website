About us

the story of VFC began with a deep love for our community and an unwavering passion for advancing health equity, diversity, and inclusion.

Our journey is rooted in the dedication of healthcare professionals who started as Certified Nursing Assistants and, through hard work and perseverance, have grown into Registered Nurses and advanced practice clinicians. This unique journey gives us a profound understanding of the healthcare system and the importance of bridging the gaps that affect both professionals and the communities they serve.

At VFC, we are driven by a commitment to closing these gaps. By supporting fellow medical professionals, healthcare organizations, and the communities around us, we work tirelessly to create meaningful change. Our mission is to attract and nurture culturally competent professionals who are trained to meet patients where they are, delivering care that respects and responds to their diverse needs.

In addition to staffing healthcare organizations with skilled professionals, we actively contribute to the broader healthcare ecosystem through clinical research support, helping to shape a more inclusive and effective future for all.

At VFC, we’re not just responding to changes in the healthcare system—we’re anticipating them.

Our passionate and experienced team is laser-focused on staying ahead of industry shifts, ensuring we provide innovative, compassionate, and reliable solutions that make a lasting impact.

Join us in our mission to create a healthier, more equitable future for everyone.
